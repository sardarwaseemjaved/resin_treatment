About Resin Bound Gravel Treatment

Resin bound gravel is a modern and attractive surface option for driveways, patios, and paths. It combines natural aggregates with a strong resin to create a permeable, durable, and visually appealing surface. This treatment is not only practical but also enhances the aesthetic appeal of your property.

Key Features

Durability

Our resin bound gravel surfaces are extremely durable and can withstand heavy usage without cracking or fading.

Permeability

The porous nature allows water to drain through, reducing puddles and promoting eco-friendly water management.

Aesthetic Appeal

Choose from a variety of colors and textures to match your style and enhance your property's curb appeal.
